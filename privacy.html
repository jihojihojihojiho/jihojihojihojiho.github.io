Privacy Policy – CarWhere

Last updated: August 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CarWhere collects precise location data (latitude, longitude, and altitude) to record and display the location of your parked vehicle. We do not collect any personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or device ID.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected location data is used solely to provide the app’s core functionality — helping you find your parked car. No data is used for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Your location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. Data may remain on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

5. Security Measures

All communications with Google Play Services APIs use industry-standard encryption (TLS). Data stored on your device is handled securely using Android’s secure storage mechanisms.

6. Children’s Privacy

CarWhere is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within this page, with the updated date shown at the top.
